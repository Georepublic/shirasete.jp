Keep an eye on your neighborhood

「知らせて.jp」 allows citizens to easily inform their local government about problems in their cities. If you have a complaint, that you want to report, just take a photo first.

Submit a report

Then write a message and submit a report with the photo attached, explaining as many details as necessary.

Help to get the issue fixed

After you submit a relevant report city staff will read and respond to your request.

Follow its progress and provide additional information if necessary.

Help to make your city a better place

Know that others and the city is listening to you, and monitor your government and ensure they do their job.
